You can attach both photos and videos to this post. Below, you will find areas to upload and manage these items.

If your browser supports HTML5, you can simply drag/drop a file to the appropriate uploader. If not, you can click the 'Add Files' button in the bottom left corner of the uploader window.

The uploaders will accept jpg, png, and gif formats for pictures. Accepted video types are mp4, webm, and ogv.

Once you have added your items, simply click the 'Start Upload' button.
